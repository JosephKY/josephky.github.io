Election Prediction Utility

Information

This utility predicts the chances of victory for the Democratic candidate, Kamala Harris, and the Republican candidate, Donald Trump, or a tie, for the 2024 United States Presidential Election. It does so based on user-defined winning chances for the Democratic and Republican candidate per-state and runs a high amount of election simulations to find the outcome averages.
